ACURA PHARMACEUTICALS HAS THE HIGHEST OPERATING MARGIN IN THE PHARMACEUTICALS INDUSTRY (ACUR, CBRX, JAZZ, XNPT, QCOR)

Print    Share

Sep 06, 2011 (SmarTrend(R) News Watch via COMTEX) -- Below are the top five companies in the Pharmaceuticals industry as ranked by operating margin. A healthy operating margin is required for a company to pay for its fixed costs and generate cash.

Acura Pharmaceuticals (NASDAQ:ACUR) had an operating margin of 85.8% in the last quarter on sales of $20.2 million.

Columbia Laboratories (NASDAQ:CBRX - Snapshot Report) had an operating margin of 81.8% in the last quarter on sales of $19.2 million.

Jazz Pharmaceuticals (NASDAQ:JAZZ - Snapshot Report) had an operating margin of 52.4% in the last quarter on sales of $64.6 million.

XenoPort (NASDAQ:XNPT - Snapshot Report) had an operating margin of 51.9% in the last quarter on sales of $37.4 million.

Questcor Pharmaceuticals (NASDAQ:QCOR - Snapshot Report) had an operating margin of 44.4% in the last quarter on sales of $46 million.

SmarTrend currently has shares of Questcor Pharmaceuticals in an Uptrend and issued the Uptrend alert on March 30, 2011 at $14.51. The stock has risen 102.2% since the Uptrend alert was issued.
