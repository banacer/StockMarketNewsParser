Stocks Hitting 52-Week Highs

By Lisa Levin
Benzinga Staff Writer
September 06, 2011 10:08 AM
Symbols: FUEL, PERF, RGLD, AUY
Tags: 52-Week Highs
Posted in: News, Intraday Update, Markets, Movers
Tweet
Share

SMF Energy Corporation (NASDAQ: FUEL) shares jumped 3.60% to create a new 52-week high of $2.59. FUEL shares have jumped 89.39% over the past 52 weeks, while the S&P 500 index has gained 6.29% in the same period.

Royal Gold Inc (NASDAQ: RGLD) shares gained 4.24% to touch a new 52-week high of $82.32. RGLD's trailing-twelve-month operating margin is 54.94%.

Perfumania Holdings Inc (NASDAQ: PERF) shares rose 1.30% to create a new 52-week high of $16.70. PERF reported its total net sales of $19.1 million for the four week fiscal month of August 2011 which ended August 27, 2011.

Yamana Gold Inc (NYSE: AUY) shares gained 2.73% to create a new 52-week high of $17.29. AUY's trailing-twelve-month revenue is $2.04 billion.
