Activision (ATVI) Trading Near $11.61 Resistance Level

Posted: Tuesday, September 06, 2011 8:02 AM EDT

Activision (NASDAQ:ATVI) closed Friday's deflating trading session at $11.33. In the past year, the stock has hit a 52-week low of $10.40 and 52-week high of $12.65. Activision (ATVI) stock has been showing support around $11.11 and resistance in the $11.61 range. Technical indicators for the stock are Bearish and S&P gives Activision (ATVI) a neutral 3 STARS (out of 5) hold rating. For a hedged play on Activision (ATVI), look at the Feb '12 $11.00 covered call for a net debit in the $10.13 area. That is also the break-even stock price for this trade. This covered call has a duration of 165 days, provides 10.59% downside protection and an assigned return rate of 8.59% for an annualized return rate of 19.00% (for comparison purposes only). A lower-cost hedged play for Activision (ATVI) would use a longer term call option in place of the covered call stock purchase. To use this strategy look at going long the Activision (ATVI) Jan '13 $7.50 call and selling the Feb '12 $11.00 call for a total debit of $3.00. The trade has a lifespan of 165 days and would provide 7.33% downside protection and an assigned return rate of 16.67% for an annualized return rate of 36.87% (for comparison purposes only). Activision (ATVI) has a current annual dividend yield of 1.39%. [ATU-Seven Summits Research]
