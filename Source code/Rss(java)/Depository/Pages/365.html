Top 5 Companies in the Restaurants Industry With the Highest Operating Margin (MCD, AFCE, SONC, DPZ, YUM)

 Posted 07:19 AM ET

Featured Stocks

  • AFCE
    A F C Enterprises Inc
    DPZ *
    Dominos Pizza Inc
    MCD *
    Mcdonalds Corp
  • SONC
    Sonic Corp
    YUM *
    Yum! Brands Inc
* Top-Rated Company
1" 2"

Sep 06, 2011 (SmarTrend(R) News Watch via COMTEX) -- Below are the top five companies in the Restaurants industry as ranked by operating margin. A healthy operating margin is required for a company to pay for its fixed costs and generate cash.

McDonald's (MCD) had an operating margin of 31.7% in the last quarter on sales of $6.9 billion.

AFC Enterprises (AFCE) had an operating margin of 27.2% in the last quarter on sales of $35.3 million.

Sonic (SONC) had an operating margin of 19.1% in the last quarter on sales of $152.1 million.

Domino's Pizza (DPZ) had an operating margin of 16.1% in the last quarter on sales of $384.9 million.

Yum! Brands (YUM) had an operating margin of 15.3% in the last quarter on sales of $2.8 billion.

SmarTrend currently has shares of AFC Enterprises in an Downtrend and issued the Downtrend alert on July 27, 2011 at $15.21. The stock has fallen 19.4% since the Downtrend alert was issued.
