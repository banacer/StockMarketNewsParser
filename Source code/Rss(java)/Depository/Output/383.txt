AMRI CFO to Present at the Rodman & Renshaw 13th Annual Healthcare
Conference




















ALBANY, N.Y., Sep 06, 2011 (BUSINESS WIRE) -- AMRI announced today that
AMRI Senior Vice President, Administration and Chief Financial Officer
Mark T. Frost will present at the Rodman & Renshaw 13th Annual Healthcare
Conference being held in New York City on Monday, September 12, 2011 at
9:35 a.m. EDT.

An audio webcast of the corporate presentation will be available to
investors and interested parties by accessing the company's website at
www.amriglobal.com . In addition, a replay of the webcast will be
available one hour after the live webcast through December 9, 2011.

Founded in 1991, Albany Molecular Research, Inc. (AMRI) provides
scientific services, products and technologies focused on improving the
quality of life. AMRI works on drug discovery and development projects
and conducts manufacturing of active ingredients and pharmaceutical
intermediates for many of the world's leading healthcare companies. As an
additional value added service to its customers, the company is also
investing in R&D in order to expand its contract services and to identify
novel early stage drug candidates with the goal to outlicense to a
strategic partner. With locations in the U.S., Europe, and Asia, AMRI
provides customers with a wide range of services, technologies and cost
models.

SOURCE: Albany Molecular Research, Inc.




        
        AMRI Investor Relations 
        Mark Frost, 518-512-2211
