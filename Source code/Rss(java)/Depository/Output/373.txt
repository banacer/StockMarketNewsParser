HIGHEST OPERATING MARGIN IN THE AIRLINES INDUSTRY DETECTED IN SHARES OF
COPA HOLDINGS (CPA, ALGT, UAL, JBLU, DAL)











Print    Share



Sep 06, 2011 (SmarTrend(R) News Watch via COMTEX) -- Below are the top
five companies in the Airlines industry as ranked by operating margin. A
healthy operating margin is required for a company to pay for its fixed
costs and generate cash.

Copa Holdings (NYSE:CPA - Snapshot Report) had an operating margin of
23.9% in the last quarter on sales of $428.5 million.

Allegiant Travel (NASDAQ:ALGT - Snapshot Report) had an operating margin
of 10.3% in the last quarter on sales of $200.4 million.

Unitedntinental Holdings (NYSE:UAL - Analyst Report) had an operating
margin of 8.2% in the last quarter on sales of $9.8 billion.

JetBlue Airways (NASDAQ:JBLU - Analyst Report) had an operating margin of
7.5% in the last quarter on sales of $1.2 billion.

Delta Air Lines (NYSE:DAL - Analyst Report) had an operating margin of
6.8% in the last quarter on sales of $9.2 billion.

SmarTrend currently has shares of Delta Air Lines in an Downtrend and
issued the Downtrend alert on June 01, 2011 at $9.88. The stock has
fallen 26.4% since the Downtrend alert was issued.
