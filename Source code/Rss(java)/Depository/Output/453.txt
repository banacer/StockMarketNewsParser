Baidu.com (BIDU) Outperforms Market On Deal To Collaborate With Dell In
China


Posted: Tuesday, September 06, 2011 10:08 AM EDT


Baidu.com (NASDAQ: BIDU) opened at $135.80. So far today, the stock has
hit a low of $135.51 and a high of $137.30. BIDU is now trading at
$137.27, down $3.18 (-2.26%). Over the last 52 weeks the stock has ranged
from a low of $82.16 to a high of $165.96. The stock is performing better
than the market this morning on news that it has agreed to work with Dell
(DELL) to develop mobile devices exclusively for the Chinese market.
Technical indicators for the stock are bullish and S&P gives BIDU a
positive 4 STARS (out of 5) buy ranking. If you are looking for a hedged
play on BIDU the stock seems like it could be a candidate for an October
out-of-the-money bull-put credit spread below the 100 range. [ABR-Seven
Summits Strategic Investments NewsBite]
