EARNINGS UPDATE: FUELCELL ENERGY RELEASES ITS QUARTERLY EARNINGS ON
SEPTEMBER 06, 2011 (FCEL)











Print    Share



Sep 05, 2011 (SmarTrend(R) News Watch via COMTEX) -- Analysts, on
average, expect FuelCell Energy (NASDAQ:FCEL - Snapshot Report) to report
a loss of $0.09 per share on sales of $29 million on September 06, 2011.

For the full year, analysts expect the company to post a loss of $0.37.
In the year-ago period, the company reported a loss of $0.15 on sales of
$19 million.

In the previous quarter, the company reported a loss of $0.19, missing
consensus estimates for a loss of $0.13.

SmarTrend currently has shares of FuelCell Energy in an Downtrend and
issued the Downtrend alert on April 19, 2011 at $1.72. The stock has
fallen 39.4% since the Downtrend alert was issued.
