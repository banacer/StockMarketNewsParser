Randgold Resources Limited (NASDAQ: GOLD) shares moved up 3.53% to
$111.81 at 10:20 am. The volume of GOLD shares traded was 91% higher than
normal. Randgold's trailing-twelve-month operating margin is 35.21%.

Harbin Electric Inc (NASDAQ: HRBN) shares rose 3.73% to $18.09. The
volume of HRBN shares traded was 53% higher than normal. HRBN's PEG ratio
is 0.56.

Silver Standard Resources Inc (NASDAQ: SSRI) shares climbed 3.59% to
$31.17. The volume of SSRI shares traded was 52% higher than normal.
SSRI's trailing-twelve-month profit margin is 213.82%.

Pharmasset Inc (NASDAQ: VRUS) shares gained 5.17% to $67.52. The volume
of VRUS shares traded was 50% higher than normal. Pharmasset announced
91% SVR12 from its phase 2b PROTON study in subjects with hepatitis C
virus (HCV) genotype 1.
