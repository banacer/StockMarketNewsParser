EARNINGS LOOK AHEAD: IN 2 DAYS, MITCHAM INDUSTRIES RELEASES ITS QUARTERLY
NUMBERS (MIND)











Print    Share



Sep 05, 2011 (SmarTrend(R) News Watch via COMTEX) -- Analysts, on
average, expect Mitcham Industries (NASDAQ:MIND - Snapshot Report) to
report earnings of $0.11 per share on sales of $20 million on September
07, 2011.

For the full year, analysts expect the company to post EPS of $1.69. In
the year-ago period, the company reported EPS of $0.04 on sales of $15
million.

In the previous quarter, the company reported EPS of $0.58, topping
consensus estimates of $0.35.

SmarTrend currently has shares of Mitcham Industries in an Downtrend and
issued the Downtrend alert on August 05, 2011 at $16.14. The stock has
fallen 5.1% since the Downtrend alert was issued.
