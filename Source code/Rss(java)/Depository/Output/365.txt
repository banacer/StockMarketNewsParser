Sep 06, 2011 (SmarTrend(R) News Watch via COMTEX) -- Below are the top
five companies in the Restaurants industry as ranked by operating margin.
A healthy operating margin is required for a company to pay for its fixed
costs and generate cash.

McDonald's (MCD) had an operating margin of 31.7% in the last quarter on
sales of $6.9 billion.

AFC Enterprises (AFCE) had an operating margin of 27.2% in the last
quarter on sales of $35.3 million.

Sonic (SONC) had an operating margin of 19.1% in the last quarter on
sales of $152.1 million.

Domino's Pizza (DPZ) had an operating margin of 16.1% in the last quarter
on sales of $384.9 million.

Yum! Brands (YUM) had an operating margin of 15.3% in the last quarter on
sales of $2.8 billion.

SmarTrend currently has shares of AFC Enterprises in an Downtrend and
issued the Downtrend alert on July 27, 2011 at $15.21. The stock has
fallen 19.4% since the Downtrend alert was issued.
