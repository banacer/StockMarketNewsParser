TOP 5 COMPANIES IN THE HEALTH CARE SUPPLIES INDUSTRY WITH THE HIGHEST
OPERATING MARGIN (ATRI, UTMD, VIVO, ANIK, NEOG)











Print    Share



Sep 06, 2011 (SmarTrend(R) News Watch via COMTEX) -- Below are the top
five companies in the Health Care Supplies industry as ranked by
operating margin. A healthy operating margin is required for a company to
pay for its fixed costs and generate cash.

Atrion (NASDAQ:ATRI) had an operating margin of 33.5% in the last quarter
on sales of $31.1 million.

Utah Medical Products (NASDAQ:UTMD) had an operating margin of 31.2% in
the last quarter on sales of $10.4 million.

Meridian Bioscience (NASDAQ:VIVO - Snapshot Report) had an operating
margin of 25.3% in the last quarter on sales of $40.1 million.

Anika Therapeutics (NASDAQ:ANIK - Snapshot Report) had an operating
margin of 22.8% in the last quarter on sales of $16.1 million.

Neogen (NASDAQ:NEOG - Snapshot Report) had an operating margin of 19.6%
in the last quarter on sales of $43.6 million.

SmarTrend currently has shares of Anika Therapeutics in an Downtrend and
issued the Downtrend alert on April 04, 2011 at $8.69. The stock has
fallen 33.1% since the Downtrend alert was issued.
