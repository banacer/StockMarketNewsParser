RELATIVELY HIGH OPERATING MARGIN DETECTED IN SHARES OF AMSURG IN THE
HEALTH CARE FACILITIES INDUSTRY (AMSG, ALC, WOOF, USPH, HLS)











Print    Share



Sep 06, 2011 (SmarTrend(R) News Watch via COMTEX) -- Below are the top
five companies in the Health Care Facilities industry as ranked by
operating margin. A healthy operating margin is required for a company to
pay for its fixed costs and generate cash.

Amsurg (NASDAQ:AMSG - Analyst Report) had an operating margin of 31.9% in
the last quarter on sales of $188.7 million.

Assisted Living Concepts (NYSE:ALC - Snapshot Report) had an operating
margin of 19.1% in the last quarter on sales of $58.6 million.

VCA Antech (NASDAQ:WOOF - Snapshot Report) had an operating margin of
18.7% in the last quarter on sales of $376.1 million.

US Physical Therapy (NASDAQ:USPH - Snapshot Report) had an operating
margin of 18.2% in the last quarter on sales of $59.9 million.

HealthSouth (NYSE:HLS - Snapshot Report) had an operating margin of 17.7%
in the last quarter on sales of $505.1 million.

SmarTrend currently has shares of Assisted Living Concepts in an
Downtrend and issued the Downtrend alert on April 14, 2011 at $18.04. The
stock has fallen 30.7% since the Downtrend alert was issued.
