CVS (CVS) (ATVI) (INSU) (BKD) (CWTR) (CSTR) Ratings Reiterated





CVS Caremark (NYSE: CVS), Activision Blizzard Inc (NASDAQ: ATVI),
Insituform Technologies, Inc. (NASDAQ: INSU), Brookdale Senior Living,
Inc. (NYSE: BKD), Coldwater Creek Inc. (NASDAQ: CWTR) and Coinstar
(NASDAQ: CSTR) had ratings on them reiterated by analysts.

Stifel Nicolaus reiterated its "Buy" rating on CVS Caremark (CVS).

Bank of America Merrill Lynch (NYSE:BAC) reiterated its "Neutral" rating
on Activision Blizzard Inc. (ATVI).

Stifel Nicolaus reiterated its "Buy" rating on Insituform Technologies,
Inc. (INSU).

Bank of America Merrill Lynch reiterated its "Buy" rating on Brookdale
Senior Living, Inc. (BKD).

Citigroup (NYSE:C) reiterated its "Hold" rating on Coldwater Creek Inc.
(CWTR).

Merriman Curhan Ford reiterated its "Buy" rating on Coinstar (CSTR).
