(RTTNews) - Elecsys Corp. (ESYS: News ) reported that its first-quarter
net income was $213,000, or $0.05 per share, up from $107,000, or $0.03
per share in the year ago quarter.

Operating income for the quarter was $389,000, compared to operating
income of $246,000 for the same quarter in the prior year.

Sales for the quarter were $5.67 million, up from $5.18 million in the
first quarter of fiscal 2011.
