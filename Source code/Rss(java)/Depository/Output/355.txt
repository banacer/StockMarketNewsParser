Active Power to Present at Ardour Capital Energy Technology Conference on
September 15




















AUSTIN, Texas, Sep 06, 2011 (BUSINESS WIRE) -- Active Power, Inc. ,
manufacturer of continuous power and infrastructure solutions, has been
invited to present at the Ardour Capital Ninth Annual Energy Technology
Conference. The conference will be held at Fordham University Graduate
School of Business, Lincoln Center Campus, in New York City, Sept. 15-16,
2011.

Active Power CFO John Penver is scheduled to present Thursday, Sept. 15,
at 3:30 p.m. Eastern time, with one-on-one meetings held throughout the
day. Penver will discuss the company's progress including second quarter
2011 results and its financial outlook for the remainder of 2011.

For the second quarter of 2011, Active Power achieved year-over-year
growth and sequential growth on a year-to-date basis with first half 2011
revenues at $36.5 million, up 35 percent compared to the same period in
2010.

This increase was largely driven by orders for the company's continuous
power and infrastructure solutions including its PowerHouse systems on a
global scale. New business in Asia and Europe also contributed to this
growth.

For more information about the conference or to schedule a one-on-one
meeting with Active Power management, please contact your Ardour Capital
representative or visit the conference Web site at
www.ardourcapital.com/annual_conference.php .

About Ardour Capital

Ardour Capital Investments, LLC provides financial services to the energy
technology sector. The firm's services include corporate finance,
business strategy, equity research, investment banking, raising capital,
corporate branding, trading, and brokerage. It focuses on small
capitalization growth companies. Ardour Capital is headquartered in New
York City. Ardour Global Indexes are a family of pure-play alternative
energy indexes. Member of FINRA/SIPC. For more information about Ardour
Capital, visit www.ardourcapital.com .

About Active Power

Founded in 1992, Active Power designs and manufactures continuous power
solutions and critical backup power systems that enable datacenters and
other mission critical operations to remain 'on' 24 hours a day, seven
days a week. Active Power solutions are intelligently efficient,
inherently reliable and economically green, providing environmental
benefits and energy and space efficiencies to customers' financial
benefit. The company's products and solutions are built with pride in
Austin, Texas, at a state-of-the-art, ISO 9001:2008 registered
manufacturing and test facility. Global customers are served via Austin
and four regional operations centers located in the United Kingdom,
Germany, Japan and China, supporting the deployment of systems in more
than 40 countries. For more information, visit www.activepower.com .
